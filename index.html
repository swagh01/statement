Stillwater Association

About

Stillwater Association is an unincorporated, member-based association. Its activities are conducted privately under written agreements among members and focus on collaborative initiatives, research and development projects, educational pursuits, and humanitarian efforts.

Our purpose is to provide a framework for members to contribute, participate, and benefit from these collective initiatives while maintaining confidentiality and respecting the private nature of our contribution to the world.
